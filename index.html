Design Thinking

An Interactive Guide

Do you have a problem that you are trying to solve?

Don't Worry!

Try to put yourself in someone else’s shoes. Think about a problem they face, that you could solve. This can be absolutely anything!

Great, tell me about it!

Do you have an idea for a prototype?

Not a problem!

Consider your problem. Brainstorm some ways that you can fix it. Don’t bother filtering at this stage, just get all of your ideas in one place.

If you’re stuck, consider asking someone else for help. Design thinking is a collaborative process!

Awesome, explain it to me!

Have you tested your prototype?

It's go time!

Now that you have a working prototype, it’s time for you to test it! Find people who experience the problem that you are trying to solve, and ask them for feedback on your prototype.

Take notes on their feedback, you will use it to improve your prototype later!
